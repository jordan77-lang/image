# Learning Objective Standards Alignment Actions

Science and research behind this tool

This generator applies established instructional design frameworks to extract or reverse-engineer measurable learning objectives and aligns them to appropriate standards.

  • Bloom's Taxonomy (Anderson & Krathwohl, 2001) for cognitive levels and measurable verbs.
  • ABCD method for learning objectives (Mager-inspired; Audience, Behavior, Condition, Degree).
  • SMART criteria for objective quality (Doran, 1981).
  • Constructive alignment principles (Biggs & Tang, 2011) to sequence objectives from foundational to higher-order.
  • NGSS three-dimensional learning (NGSS Lead States, 2013) for science practices and performance expectations.
  • CCSS (ELA/Math) for literacy and quantitative reasoning in disciplinary tasks.
  • LLM-assisted text analysis: objectives are extracted or inferred from assignment tasks, steps, and assessments using structured prompting and JSON-constrained outputs.
